Frequently Asked Questions

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings ‚Üí Data Management ‚Üí Export Data. You can export your transactions as a CSV file for backup or analysis.

Is my data secure?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device using Core Data. We don't collect or store any personal information on external servers.

How do I set up budgets?

Navigate to the Budget tab and tap the "+" button to create a new budget. Set your spending limit and track your progress throughout the month.

Can I use Nuvio offline?

Absolutely! Nuvio works completely offline. All your data is stored locally on your device, so you can track your finances anywhere, anytime.
